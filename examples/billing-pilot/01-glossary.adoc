# Billing Pilot Glossary @doc(billing.glossary)

Shared billing language for the V0 pilot.

::glossary billing.credits
owner: team-billing
--
Credits are account balance adjustments that reduce future invoices after a payment, refund, or support correction.
::

::glossary billing.ledger
owner: team-billing
--
The billing ledger is the append-only record of money movement for invoices, payments, credits, refunds, and adjustments.
::

::glossary billing.invoice
owner: team-billing
--
An invoice is the customer-facing statement of charges, taxes, credits, and the remaining amount due for an account.
::

::glossary billing.refund
owner: team-billing
--
A refund returns money to a payment method and must be paired with ledger entries that explain the account balance change.
::

::glossary billing.entitlement
owner: team-access
--
An entitlement is the product access state granted by paid invoices, trials, credits, or account-level overrides.
::

::glossary billing.settlement
owner: team-billing
--
Settlement is the point when the payment processor confirms captured money can update account balance and entitlement state.
::

::glossary billing.adjustment
owner: finance-ops
--
An adjustment is a manual or automated correction that changes invoice balance outside the normal payment or refund path.
::
